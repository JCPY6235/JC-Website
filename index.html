About Me

I'm a dedicated PhD student pursuing advanced studies in Quantitative Modelling at UM6P (Morocco), with a strong foundation in analysis, numerical methods, and mathematical modeling.

My passion lies in applied mathematics and developing innovative computational solutions. I specialize in:

  • Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)
  • Domain Decomposition Methods
  • High-Performance Computing (MPI, Parallelization)
  • Numerical Methods for PDEs
  • Machine Learning & Scientific Computing

I thrive in collaborative environments that encourage innovation and continuous learning, aiming to contribute impactful research to scientific and industrial contexts.

Research Focus

Physics-Informed Neural Networks

Integrating PINNs with domain decomposition methods. Recent work: modified Helmholtz equation with 40-50% convergence improvement using Robin transmission conditions.

High-Performance Computing

Parallel algorithms using MPI, domain decomposition, and distributed computing. Experience with 2D Poisson solvers and stochastic gradient optimization.

Numerical Methods for PDEs

Developing and analyzing finite volume schemes, semi-Lagrangian methods, and Runge-Kutta solvers for complex differential equations.

Model Reduction & Optimization

Model reduction for PDEs with constraints, parametrized variational inequalities, and contact mechanics applications.
